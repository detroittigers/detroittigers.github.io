Delete Your Ride Ready Account

There are two ways to delete your account. Both work. Pick whichever is easier.

Option 1: In the app (fastest)

  1. Open Ride Ready
  2. Tap Account
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm. Your data is removed immediately.

Option 2: Submit a request here

Enter your account email below. You'll get a confirmation email within 30 days once it's done.

What we delete

  • Profile (name, email, sign-in identity)
  • Ride logs and history
  • Day plans and saved plans
  • Alerts and notification subscriptions
  • Favorites and avoid list
  • Park check-in history
  • Push notification tokens

What we keep

  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics that cannot be tied to you
  • Records required by law (currently none)

Cancel your subscription separately

Deleting your account does not cancel an active subscription. Cancel through whichever store you subscribed through:

  • Google Play: play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions
  • App Store: apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions
