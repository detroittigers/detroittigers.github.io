Planning Your Day at Islands of Adventure

Islands of Adventure hosts two of the most heavily themed queues in Orlando. Hagrid's Motorbike winds through a recreated Forbidden Forest and ruined Scottish castle. VelociCoaster's Raptor Paddock turns the line into a pre-ride story beat. Both queues are built to disguise how long you've been waiting.

Our guides tell you exactly how much time you have left based on the room or landmark around you, so you can plan snack breaks, restroom runs, and alert settings without guessing.

Pro Tip: Islands of Adventure is LIVE in the Ride Ready app. Set a drop alert and only join the line when the wait is actually worth it.
