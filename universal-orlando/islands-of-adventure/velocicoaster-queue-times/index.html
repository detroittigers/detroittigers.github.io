⚠️ Queue Survival Tips

  • Lockers Are Mandatory: All loose articles go in the free dual-sided lockers mid-queue. You'll pass through metal detectors after.
  • Lap Bars Only: No over-the-shoulder restraints. If you're nervous, sit in an interior seat (rows 2-5).
  • Single Rider: Opens intermittently. When available, it can cut your wait to 10-20 minutes. Check with a team member at the entrance.
  • Express Pass: Accepted. Merges near the metal detectors (~14 min mark).
  • Back Row = Best Row: Row 6 (back) gives the most intense airtime on the top hat and inversions. Front row has the best views but often a longer sub-queue.

Ride Stats

Manufactured by Intamin and opened June 10, 2021, VelociCoaster is a steel launched coaster with two launches (0-50 mph in 2 seconds, then 40-70 mph in 2.4 seconds), 4 inversions including a 100-foot zero-gravity stall, a 155-foot top hat, a 140-foot 80-degree drop, and 4,700 feet of track. Universal promises 12 seconds of airtime.

Why is the queue so immersive?

Universal designed VelociCoaster's queue as a narrative experience rather than a holding pen. You progress from the paddock exterior through the research facility to a face-to-face raptor encounter before getting a "safety briefing" from two movie stars. The scent emitters, animatronic raptors, and augmented-reality windows make it one of the best queues in Orlando — even when the wait is long.
