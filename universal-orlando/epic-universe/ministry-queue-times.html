Why is the line so deceptive?

Epic Universe queues are designed as immersive "scenes" rather than visible switchbacks. This kills the guest's ability to gauge time. A user standing in the Ministry of Magic "Map Room" has no idea if they have 10 minutes or 60 minutes left. This guide helps you plan your breaks and manage expectations.
