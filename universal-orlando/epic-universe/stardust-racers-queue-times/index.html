  • Motion Warning: High-speed coaster with inversions and airtime. Lap restraints only means you feel every G-force.
  • Green vs. Yellow: Pulsar (Green) is more intense with airtime. Photon (Yellow) is smoother.
  • Apple Watch Trick: Metal detectors trip on watches. Raise hands above head AND cover watch face to avoid getting pulled aside.
  • Weather: As an outdoor coaster, this closes instantly for lightning. Check the app.

The No-Phone Zone

Stardust Racers has a strict loose article policy. Unlike other rides where you can stow items in pouches, here you must use a locker well before the boarding area. This means you will be standing in line for 20-25 minutes without your device. Plan accordingly!

Best Times: Rope drop (10-20 min), before 11:30 AM, or the final 30 minutes. Pro Tip: Ride at night to see the portal lights converge with the Cosmos Fountain show!
