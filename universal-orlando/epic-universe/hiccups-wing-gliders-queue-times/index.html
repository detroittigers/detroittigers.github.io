‚ö†Ô∏è Know Before You Queue

  • DON'T Save For Last: This ride hits capacity near closing. Many guests miss it entirely by waiting too late.
  • Slow Loading: 75+ min average wait. The ride loads slowly, so waits often exceed 2 hours on busy days.
  • Sun Exposure: Outdoor queue with netting but still hot. Bring sunscreen, hat, and water.
  • Family-Friendly: Similar to Slinky Dog Dash. Two gentle launches, no inversions. Most motion-sensitive guests handle it fine.

The Locker Warning

Correction: Unlike Stardust Racers, this ride does NOT have metal detectors. However, the "No Loose Articles" policy is still strictly enforced by staff. Use the free lockers at the 20-minute mark to avoid being turned away at the station.

Best Times: Rope drop is essential for short waits. Also try mealtimes or use Express Pass. Pro Tip: This ride often stays open past official closing time - but if the wait is long near close, they may stop admitting guests.
