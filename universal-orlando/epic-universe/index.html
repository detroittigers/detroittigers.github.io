Planning Your Day at Epic Universe

Universal Epic Universe introduces a new era of theme park queues. Many lines are indoors and heavily themed, making it difficult to judge wait times visually. Our guides help you identify exactly where you are in line based on the room and props around you.

Pro Tip: Epic Universe support is LIVE in the Ride Ready app. Download it today to track wait times.
