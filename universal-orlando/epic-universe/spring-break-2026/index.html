Early Entry: What's Actually Opening

March 8–22, 2026 · 16 days tracked · Updated Mar 23

Universal's official EPA page lists three lands but no specific rides or times. Here's what we've tracked every morning of spring break so far.

Ride Typical Open Reliable? First Wait On EPA Page?
Dragon Racer's Rally ~8:00 AM Most days 5–15 min Yes
Hiccup's Wing Gliders ~8:00 AM Most days 5–25 min Yes
Monsters Unchained ~8:10 AM Every day 10 min No
Mine-Cart Madness ~8:15 AM Hit or miss 60–120 min Yes
Mario Kart ~8:05 AM Every day 0–130 min Yes
Battle at the Ministry ~8:15 AM Improving 60–120 min Yes
Curse of the Werewolf ~8:50 AM Every day, late 5–10 min No

Ministry is getting more reliable. Week 1 was a coin flip (7:58 AM one day, 9:30 the next). Week 2 it opened 5 of 6 days, typically by 8:15 AM. Still not worth banking on at rope drop, but the trend's looking better. Have a backup either way.

Mario Kart is the most reliable early entry headliner, opening every single day of spring break so far, typically by 8:05 AM. Monsters and Werewolf aren't on Universal's EPA page but both open every morning. HTTYD rides consistently open before 8 AM.

    Our planner saved this for last in 90% of early entry days. 150 min at peak → 39 min by 9 PM. The 74% evening collapse is the most dramatic of any headliner. Never waste it at midday.
    Insider note: We tracked 168 park days and found Ministry closes early on roughly 1 in 9 nights. A team member told us on busy days they may cut the line before close, and if waits hit 3+ hours during the day, an early shutdown is more likely. The move: get in line by 8 PM to be safe. If it's been showing 3-hour waits all day, don't bank on riding it last.

Monsters Unchained: The Hidden Walk-On

51,906 baseline samples · Oct 2025–Mar 22, 2026

20 min Median wait, all hours
33 min Peak median (11 AM)
7× Shorter than Mine-Cart

Across 51,906 baseline samples from 168 park days, Monsters Unchained shows a profile unlike any other headliner at Epic Universe. Its average median wait is 20 minutes, compared to 57–134 min for other headliners during the same hours. Its peak median is only 33 min (11 AM–noon). That's shorter than Dragon Racer's Rally or Stardust Racers at their noon peak.

After 7 PM, the median drops to 14 min or less and stays there until close. On weekends at 8 PM, the median is 13 min, a walk-on by any reasonable definition, even when Mine-Cart is posting 120 min and Battle at Ministry is at 79 min.

Real talk: Every other headliner at Epic Universe has a time window when you should avoid it. Monsters Unchained has no such window. The ride most guests treat as a fallback is actually more efficiently designed than rides half the park is skipping entirely. Use it anytime, especially during the noon–2 PM peak when everything else is at maximum pain.
