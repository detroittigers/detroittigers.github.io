The 3 Rules

1. Pick Sunday. If you only have one day, Sunday saves you 40+ minutes on Ministry alone vs Monday.
2. Hit Mario Kart at 9 AM. It opens first during EPA. By 10:30, it's 90+ minutes. By noon, it's 2 hours.
3. Ride headliners after 7 PM. Ministry drops from 126 min to under 60. Stardust hits 27 min at 9 PM.
Don't trust Monday. Federal holiday = schools/banks closed. Waits spike 25%+. The "extra day" isn't worth it.

The Perfect MLK Sunday

Hour-by-Hour Timeline
RideReady.app
7:45 AM Arrive at parking. Queue at gate. Bring layers (50-60°F mornings). You want to be walking in at 8:55.
9:00 AM EPA Opens → Mario Kart. Walk directly to Super Nintendo World. Don't stop. Expected: 45-60 min (vs 90+ by 10:30).
10:15 AM Exit Mario Kart → Yoshi's Adventure while you're in Nintendo. 35-40 min wait.
11:00 AM Head to Celestial Park. Grab Starfall Racers or explore. Waits are climbing park-wide.
11:30 AM LUNCH BREAK. This is peak wait time. Eat now. Biscuit Bombs in Nintendo or Three Broomsticks in Dark Universe.
12:30 PM Shows + Light Rides. This is NOT the time for headliners. Catch a show in Dark Universe or explore Isle of Berk.
1-4 PM Flexible window. Dragon Racer (38 min), Hiccup's (47 min), Monsters Unchained (24 min). Or rest at the hotel if multi-day.
5:00 PM Mine-Cart Madness. Crowds thinning. Harry Potter fans leaving. Expected: 50-65 min.
6:30 PM Dinner decision: Eat now OR skip dinner and ride during the dinner exodus. Your call.
7:30 PM Ministry of Magic. This is your moment. Waits drop to 60-70 min range. Get in line.
8:30 PM Stardust Racers re-ride. 27 min average at 9 PM. One more lap before close.
9:00 PM Night photos. Final laps. The park is beautiful after dark. Mario Kart often has short waits now too.
