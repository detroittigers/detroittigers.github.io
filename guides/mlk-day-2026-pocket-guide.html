Optimal Day Timeline

The Perfect MLK Sunday

7:45 AM Arrive at parking. Queue at gate. Bring layers for chilly mornings.
9:00 AM EPA OPENS → Mario Kart. Head directly there. Expected wait: 45-60 min (vs 90+ later).
10:15 AM Exit Mario Kart → Ministry of Magic (if open) or Monsters Unchained.
11:30 AM LUNCH BREAK. Eat during peak wait times. Nintendo World or Celestial Park.
12:30 PM Explore. Shows, light rides. This is NOT the time for headliners.
1-4 PM Flexible time: Yoshi's (42 min), Stardust (32 min), Dragon Racer (38 min), shows.
5:00 PM Mine-Cart Madness. Evening crowds thinning. Expected: 45-60 min.
6:30 PM Dinner break (or keep riding during dinner exodus).
7:30 PM Ministry re-ride. Waits drop to 60-70 min range.
8:30 PM Stardust Raceway. 27 min avg at 9 PM.
9:00 PM Night photos. Final laps. The park is beautiful after dark.
