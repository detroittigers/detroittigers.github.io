The February Sweet Spot

Presidents Day is the lightest major weekend of the year. Christmas crowds are gone, spring break hasn't started. You can actually sleep in. Arrive by 9:30 AM and still have a great day.

3 Rules for Presidents Day

1. Sleep in. You don't need 7:30 AM rope drop. February crowds are manageable. Arrive 9:30 AM.
2. Ride during dinner. Valentine's Day = packed 6-8 PM dinner reservations. Lines shrink.
3. Skip Express Pass. February crowds don't justify $160-250. Smart timing wins.

Why It's Quiet

  • Holiday exhaustion - New Year's crowds long gone
  • Park maturity - 7+ months of operations
  • Weather perfection - 70s, low humidity
  • Pre-spring break - March rush hasn't started

Valentine's Day Advantage (Saturday, Feb 14)

Who's there: More couples, fewer families with young kids. Romantic evening atmosphere.

The hidden edge: Everyone has 6-8 PM dinner reservations. Ride lines shrink during dinner rush.

Pro tip: Book YOUR dinner for 7:30 PM. Ride headliners 5-7 PM while everyone else eats.

Romantic rides: Stardust Raceway at sunset. Ministry of Magic after dark.

Quick Reference

Express Pass Verdict

Skip it.

February crowds don't justify $160-250. Smart timing gets you the same result for free.

Only consider if:

  • Less than 6 hours in park
  • Mobility limitations
  • Money is truly no object

Weather Expectations
