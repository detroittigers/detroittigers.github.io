The Relaxed Strategy

Unlike Christmas "commando touring," February allows a relaxed approach.

9:30 AM Arrive. Yes, really. Skip the rope drop crush.
10:00 AM Second-tier rides. Yoshi's, Dragon Racer, Monsters. Lines are short.
12:00 PM Headliner #1. Mario Kart or Mine-Cart. Expected: 60-75 min.
1:00 PM LUNCH. Eat while others ride. Opposite of peak-day strategy.
2-5 PM Explore freely. Shows, hidden details, character meets, re-rides.
6:00 PM Headliner #2. Ministry or Mario Kart. Valentine's dinner exodus starts.
8:00 PM Re-rides and night photos. Park is beautiful after dark.

Valentine's Day Bonus (Saturday, Feb 14)

Who's there: More couples, fewer families with young kids. Romantic evening atmosphere.

The advantage: Dinner reservations at 6-8 PM = shorter ride lines.

Pro tip: Book dinner for 7 PM. Ride headliners 5-7 PM while others eat.
