Quick Decision

Can choose dates? → Pick Week 5, 6, or 7. Post-Easter is golden.

Stuck with Week 2? → Express Pass. No question. $250 saves your sanity.

Week 3? → Smart timing works. Sunday is best. Express optional.

Week 4 (Easter)? → Express recommended. Mon/Tue are your friends.

Week 2 Collision (Mar 16-22): ALL Florida public schools + Dallas-Fort Worth (1M students) + Ontario Canada (2M students) + major universities. This is the absolute worst week.
Easter Week (Week 4): Easter Sunday is April 5. Good Friday is April 3. Easter travelers + Michigan spring break overlap. Crowds jump to 9/10.

Park-by-Park Tips

Epic Universe

EPA (Hotel guests): 1 hour early. Mario Kart opens first.

Ride Priority:

  1. Mario Kart: Bowser (rope drop)
  2. Ministry of Magic (10:30 AM or 7 PM+)
  3. Mine-Cart Madness (evening)
  4. Everything else as time allows

Evening Strategy:

5 PM Mine-Cart (thinning)
7 PM Ministry re-ride
8 PM Stardust (27 min avg)

Islands of Adventure

Priority Order:

  1. Hagrid's Motorbike (rope drop or lunch)
  2. VelociCoaster
  3. Forbidden Journey

Hagrid's tip: Single rider line often 30 min when standby is 120+

Universal Studios

Priority Order:

  1. Revenge of the Mummy
  2. Transformers

Disney World

Genie+ vs Universal Express:

Genie+ Universal
Price $25-45/day $109-349
How Book 1 at a time Unlimited skips

Park hopping: Can't hop until 2 PM. Plan accordingly.

SeaWorld Orlando

The Hidden Gem: Usually 30-40% lighter than Disney/Universal. Quick Queue rarely needed except Week 2. Even Week 2 is manageable here.

Packing Checklist

Must-Have:

  • Portable phone charger (you'll use apps all day)
  • Comfortable walking shoes (10+ miles/day)
  • Sunscreen SPF 50 (Florida sun is brutal)
  • Light rain jacket/poncho
  • Refillable water bottle

Nice-to-Have:

  • Small backpack
  • Cooling towel (April weeks)
  • Portable fan
  • Change of socks (water rides)

Key Takeaways

AVOID: Week 2 (Mar 16-22) unless you have Express Pass.
WATCH OUT: Week 4 is Easter. Higher crowds than expected.
BEST VALUE: Weeks 5-7 (post-Easter). Low crowds, great weather, no Express needed.
EVERY DAY: Rope drop + dinner strategy. Get headliners at 9 AM and 7 PM.
