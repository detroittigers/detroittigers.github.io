Park-Specific Tips

Epic Universe

Early Park Admission (EPA): Hotel guests get 1 hour early. Mario Kart opens first.

Ride Priority:

  1. Mario Kart: Bowser (rope drop)
  2. Ministry of Magic (10:30 AM or 7 PM+)
  3. Mine-Cart Madness (evening)
  4. Everything else as time allows

Evening Strategy:

5 PM Mine-Cart (thinning)
7 PM Ministry re-ride
8 PM Stardust (27 min avg)

Universal Orlando

Hagrid's Strategy: Rope drop or ride during lunch. Backup: single rider.

Islands of Adventure Priority:

  1. Hagrid's Motorbike Adventure
  2. VelociCoaster
  3. Forbidden Journey

Universal Studios Priority:

  1. Revenge of the Mummy
  2. Transformers

Disney World

Genie+ vs Universal Express:

Genie+ Universal
Price $25-45/day $109-349
How Book 1 at a time Unlimited skips

Park Hopping: Can't hop until 2 PM. Plan accordingly.

SeaWorld Orlando

The Hidden Gem: Usually 30-40% lighter than Disney/Universal. Quick Queue rarely needed except Week 2. Even Week 2 is manageable here.

Packing Checklist

Must-Have:

  • Portable phone charger
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Sunscreen (Florida sun!)
  • Light rain jacket/poncho
  • Refillable water bottle

Nice-to-Have:

  • Small backpack
  • Cooling towel (April)
  • Portable fan
  • Change of socks

Key Takeaways

Avoid: Week 2 (Mar 16-22) unless you have Express Pass.
Watch Out: Week 4 is Easter. Higher crowds than expected.
Best Value: Weeks 5-7 (post-Easter). Low crowds, great weather.
