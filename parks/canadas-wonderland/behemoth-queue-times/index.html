Tips for Behemoth

  • Sprint to Behemoth at gate drop. It's about an 8-minute walk from International Street through the bridge into Action Zone. The 10-minute median at 10 AM is the biggest single-ride window in the park.
  • From Behemoth's exit, walk five minutes to Frontier Canada for Yukon Striker (15 min at this point), then a few steps further for Mighty Canadian Minebuster (5 min). Three majors banked in 90 minutes.
  • Avoid the 3-5 PM window. The afternoon peak is the steadiest 40-45-min wall in the data.
  • 9-10 PM is the second great window. The evening collapse is reliable: 28 at 9 PM, 15 at 10 PM. Cooler air means a slightly faster ride too.
  • Aim for the back row if you can. The fifth camelback hill ejects hardest from the back, the hammerhead turnaround over Swan Lake feels closer to the water.

FAQ

When should I ride Behemoth at Canada's Wonderland?

Rope drop. Behemoth opens at a 10-minute median and climbs to 45 by 4 PM. The 35-minute swing between gate drop and peak is the biggest single-ride savings at the park. The second window is the closing collapse: 28 at 9 PM and 15 at 10 PM.

How tall is Behemoth?

230 feet tall, 77 mph, with 5,318 feet of layout. It's a Bolliger & Mabillard hyper coaster, opened 2008. Behemoth was Canada's biggest ride investment at the time, at CAD $26 million. It was the tallest coaster in Canada until Leviathan opened in 2012.

What makes Behemoth different from other hyper coasters?

Behemoth was the prototype for B&M's V-seat trains, designed to give every rider an unobstructed view forward. The five camelback hills deliver more than 20 seconds of sustained airtime, and the hammerhead turnaround skims 15 feet above the Swan Lake fountain jets at the entrance.

Behemoth or Leviathan, which should I ride first?

Behemoth, every time. Behemoth at gate drop is a 10-minute wait. Leviathan at gate drop is 30, and Leviathan's second hour spikes to 60. Hit Behemoth first while it's at single digits, then walk to Yukon Striker. Loop back to Leviathan after lunch or save it for the closing hour where it drops to 30.
