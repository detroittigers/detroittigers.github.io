Tips for Yukon Striker

  • Rope drop is the cleanest window. After Behemoth, walk straight to Frontier Canada. Yukon Striker sits at 15 min. Mighty Canadian Minebuster (5 min) is right next to it for the third leg of the morning sweep.
  • Avoid the 4-5 PM window unless you have Fast Lane Plus. The afternoon peak is the most consistent in the data.
  • The 9-10 PM window is the second clear opportunity, drops back to 20-30 min.
  • If you can ride in the front row, do it. The three-second hold at the top of the 245-ft drop is the entire experience and the front row gets the best Toronto skyline view before release.
  • Ride was excavated under the existing lake; thousands of fish were relocated during construction. The underwater tunnel section is not visible from the queue.

FAQ

When should I ride Yukon Striker?

Rope drop is the strongest window: 15 min at 10 AM versus 45 at 4 PM. The second window is 9-10 PM where it drops back to 20-30. Yukon Striker is part of the classic Canada's Wonderland morning sweep: Behemoth, then walk to Frontier Canada for Yukon Striker and Mighty Canadian Minebuster.

How tall is Yukon Striker's drop?

245 feet, with a 90-degree vertical drop angle. The trains hold riders face-down at the top for three seconds before release. It's the only dive coaster in the world with a full vertical loop and dives under the lake through an excavated tunnel where thousands of fish were relocated during construction.

What records does Yukon Striker hold?

When it opened in 2019, Yukon Striker held the height, speed, and length records for the dive coaster class. It remains the only dive coaster with a full vertical loop. Top speed is 80 mph and total length is 3,625 feet.

Is Yukon Striker scarier than other dive coasters?

It's the only dive coaster with a full vertical loop, so the inversion experience is unique to this model. The 245-ft drop is one of the longest in the dive-coaster class. If you've ridden Valravn (Cedar Point) or Griffon (Busch Gardens Williamsburg), Yukon Striker matches Valravn's 223-ft tower height but pairs a faster top speed and a deeper drop with an inversion those two dive coasters don't have.
