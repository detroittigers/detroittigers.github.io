FAQ

When should I ride The Beast at night?

The Beast night ride is legendary, but it pushes the line up. Waits hit 45 minutes at 9 PM. Get in line by 7:30 PM to ride in near-darkness without the worst of the closing rush.

How long is The Beast?

The Beast is 7,361 feet long, making it the world's longest wooden roller coaster. The ride traverses 35 acres of forest and includes a famous 540-degree helix tunnel finale.

Why does The Beast line get longer at night?

The Beast's night ride through pitch-black forest is one of the best coaster experiences anywhere. Demand surges as the sun sets. Waits jump from 34 min at 6 PM to 45 min at 9 PM as riders specifically save it for the dark.
