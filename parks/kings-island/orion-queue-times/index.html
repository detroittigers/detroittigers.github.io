FAQ

When is Orion shortest?

Orion averages just 19 minutes at 10 AM when the park opens. Lines climb steadily through the afternoon and spike hard at closing, reaching 41 minutes at 9 PM.

Does Orion have a closing rush?

Yes. Orion's line spikes to 41 minutes at 9 PM as guests rush for one last ride. If you want a short wait, ride before noon or accept the closing rush and get in line by 8:30 PM.

Is Orion a true giga coaster?

Yes. Orion has a 300-foot drop, making it a true giga coaster by the standard definition (drop of 300+ feet). It reaches 91 mph and stands 287 feet tall.
