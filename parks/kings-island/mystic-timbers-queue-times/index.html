FAQ

What's in the shed at Mystic Timbers?

The shed is the surprise ending of Mystic Timbers. We won't spoil it here, but it cycles through multiple scenarios, so you may get a different experience each time you ride. It's part of what makes Mystic Timbers so re-rideable.

When is Mystic Timbers shortest?

Mystic Timbers averages just 18 minutes at 10 AM when the park opens. The line peaks at 37 minutes around 3 PM, then drops back to 29 minutes by 9 PM.

Is Mystic Timbers rough?

No. Mystic Timbers is a GCI wooden coaster known for being smooth and fast. GCI coasters use Millennium Flyer trains that track well through turns. It's one of the smoothest wooden coasters you'll find.
