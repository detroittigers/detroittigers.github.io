🌲 The Beast Night Ride: Timing the Legend

The Beast at night is one of the most iconic experiences in all of theme parks: 7,359 feet of wooden track through the pitch-black woods. But everyone knows this, and the data shows it. Unlike every other ride at Kings Island, The Beast gets worse as the park closes.

26 min 10 AM (rope drop)
36 min 5 PM
45 min 9 PM (close)

The Beast is one of the only rides in our entire dataset where the closing hour is the longest wait of the day. At 8 PM it's 44 min; at 9 PM it hits 45 min, the daily peak. Everyone is rushing back to Rivertown for one last ride through the woods in the dark.

The play: Get in line by 7:30–8:00 PM at the latest. The queue builds fast as sunset approaches. If you wait until 9 PM, you'll be in the longest line The Beast sees all day. The night ride is worth it. Just time it right.

🗺️ Rope Drop Strategy

Optimized to minimize total wait time across all major attractions, with The Beast night ride built into the plan.

10:00 AM · Park opens
Flight of Fear. 36 min at 10 AM vs 65 min at 3 PM. Indoor LIM launch coaster with low capacity. The worst afternoon waits in the park. Always first.
10:30 AM
Orion. Same area (Area 72), walk right over. 19 min at 10 AM. Doubles to 38 min by 3 PM and spikes to 41 min at close.
11:00 AM
Banshee. Head to Action Zone. 30 min at 11 AM, stays steady all day. Better to knock it out now while you're in the area.
Midday
Mystic Timbers, Diamondback, Racer, Adventure Express. All manageable midday. Mix in flat rides like Delirium (17 min avg) and Adventure Express (16 min avg). Both are near walk-ons.
12:00 – 3:00 PM · Soak City
Soak City water park. Included with admission. Coaster waits peak 12–3 PM. Escape to the water park and come back when crowds thin.
7:30 PM
Get in line for The Beast. Night ride demand builds fast: 44 min at 8 PM, 45 min at 9 PM. Line up by 7:30 to beat the closing rush.
After Beast
Diamondback or Banshee. Both drop 21-33% in the evening as crowds rush to Beast and Orion. Perfect closers while everyone else is in Rivertown and Area 72.
The key insight: Flight of Fear first, Beast last, water park in the middle. This simple structure saves 60+ minutes of waiting across a full day compared to winging it.
