Phantom Theater: Opening Nightmare

An interactive dark ride from Sally Dark Rides opened April 18, 2026 in Planet Snoopy, 26 scenes with spellbound flashlights, animatronic opera performers, and Easter eggs to the original 1992 Phantom Theater that occupied the same building. Rides worth setting alerts for

Orion

B&M giga coaster - Area 72
287 ft91 mph5,321 ft300-ft drop

19 min at rope drop, 50 min at close. Alerts catch any mid-day dip so you can double-ride without eating a 60-min line.

Fun fact: One of only seven giga coasters in the world. The 300-foot drop is the fifth-longest on any coaster on Earth.

The Beast

Wooden coaster - Rivertown
7,361 ft64.8 mphWorld's longest wood

Evening riser, 26 min at open, 65 at close. Alerts catch the afternoon trough for a second ride without the night-ride wait.

Fun fact: Opened 1979. Still holds the Guinness record for longest wooden coaster on Earth, 47 years running.

Flight of Fear

Indoor LIM coaster - Area 72
Indoor54 mph launch4 inversions

36 min at open, peaks at 65 by 3 PM, drops back to 36 by 9 PM. Alerts catch the mid-day peak so you don't walk into it blind.

Fun fact: First roller coaster on Earth to use a LIM launch system, opened 1996. Entire ride happens indoors in pitch dark.

Mystic Timbers

GCI wood coaster - Rivertown
109 ft53 mph3,265 ft

Highest-rated ride in the park. Climbs from 18 at open to 37 at 3 PM and never drops back. Alerts catch rare dips.

Fun fact: Ride finale is an enclosed shed with randomized creature projections, the "What's in the shed?" reveal. Won 2017 Golden Ticket for Best New Ride.

Diamondback

B&M hyper coaster - Rivertown
230 ft80 mph5,282 ft

Climbs from 18 at open to 39 at 3 PM, then plateaus. Alerts catch the late-day plateau dips for a re-ride.

Fun fact: Splashdown finale sends water 50 feet in the air. Open-air stadium seating trains designed by B&M.

Banshee

B&M inverted - Action Zone
167 ft68 mph7 inversionsWorld's longest inverted

Flat all day (17–36 min range), no strategic advantage to timing. Use as mid-day filler between targeted rides.

Fun fact: Still the world's longest inverted coaster at 4,124 feet. Built on the land vacated by Son of Beast's demolition.

The Bat

Suspended coaster - Action Zone
78 ft51 mphSwinging

Classic evening dropper, 37 at 1 PM drops to 19 by 9 PM. Set an alert after 6 PM to catch the dip.

Fun fact: Trains swing freely side-to-side as they bank through the woods. Opened 1993 as "Top Gun," renamed in 2014 to honor Arrow's original 1981 Bat that stood on similar terrain.

If you only have one day

  1. Sprint to Orion at gate drop. Area 72 is in the back, don't dawdle. 19 min at open vs 50 at close.
  2. Walk to Rivertown for the three-ride sweep. The Beast, then Mystic Timbers, then Diamondback, all under 30 min if you hustle.
  3. Mid-day: Action Zone + Coney Mall sweeps. Banshee, Drop Tower, Delirium, Racers, Queen City, WindSeeker. All flat.
  4. After 7 PM: Evening droppers. The Bat, Invertigo, then queue Flight of Fear at 9 PM for the biggest drop in the park.
  5. Don't save Orion or Beast for late. Kings Island inverts the usual rule, those two rise into the evening, not drop.

FAQ

When does Kings Island open for the 2026 season?

The 2026 regular season opens Friday, April 18, 2026 (passholder preview April 17). Weekends only through mid-May, then daily operation through Labor Day. Halloween Haunt runs weekends late September and October. Winterfest typically runs mid-November through early January.

Is Fast Lane Plus worth it at Kings Island?

It depends on your crowd day. On weekdays or during shoulder season, rope drop gets you Orion and the Rivertown coasters at standby. On Saturdays, Haunt nights, and peak summer days, Fast Lane Plus pays for itself on Orion and Flight of Fear alone. Check live waits the morning of, if Orion is posting 45+ min at open, FLP is worth it.

What makes Kings Island different from Cedar Point?

Cedar Point is a coaster destination, 18 coasters, world-class density, everything is a headliner. Kings Island is a more balanced regional park with the world's longest wooden coaster (Beast), a giga coaster (Orion), and strong mid-tier rides. Kings Island's big quirk: Orion and Beast are evening risers. Cedar Point follows the normal "save headliners for evening" rule; Kings Island inverts it.
