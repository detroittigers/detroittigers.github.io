Rides worth setting alerts for

Orion

Giga coaster • park's marquee attraction
287 ft91 mph5,321 ft300 ft drop

The 7th giga coaster ever built. Consistently the #1 wait in the park. Alerts let you grab it without burning your morning.

Fun fact: $30M investment that anchors the Area 72 aerospace-themed section.

The Beast

World's longest wooden coaster • night ride legend
7,361 ft65 mph35 acres540° helix

The Beast at night, through 35 acres of forest at 65 mph in total darkness, is a bucket-list coaster experience. Time it with alerts so you're in line before sunset.

Fun fact: Guinness World Record holder since 1979. Built in-house by Kings Island engineers. Two lift hills.

Banshee

World's longest inverted coaster • 7 inversions
167 ft68 mph4,124 ft7 inversions

World's longest inverted coaster and world's fastest non-launched invert. Lines build fast. Catch the dips with alerts.

Fun fact: Named after a wailing spirit of Irish mythology. The on-ride audio delivers.

Mystic Timbers

GCI wooden • the "shed" mystery
109 ft53 mph3,265 ft

Consistently beloved by enthusiasts. The famous "shed" ending drives re-rides. Easy to stack multiple laps if you time it right.

Fun fact: With The Beast and Racer, Kings Island has more wooden coaster footage than any park on Earth.

Diamondback

B&M hypercoaster • 10 drops + splashdown
230 ft80 mph5,282 ft

High-capacity B&M trains keep the line moving faster than it looks. Great "bonus ride" between headliners.

Fun fact: First B&M coaster at Kings Island. The splashdown finale soaks spectators on the bridge.

Phantom Theater: Opening Nightmare

Interactive dark ride • NEW 2026
Sally Dark Rides26 scenesOpening Apr 18

Revival of the beloved 1992-2002 classic. Expect elevated demand all season. Track it so you catch short windows.

Fun fact: Riders collect "ghost notes" from a haunted pipe organ to prevent an opening night disaster.

If you only have one day

  1. Head straight to Orion in Area 72 at rope drop. It builds the longest lines fastest.
  2. Banshee next (Action Zone) while morning crowds are still thin.
  3. Mystic Timbers and Flight of Fear before noon. Both have slow-loading ops that punish afternoon visitors.
  4. Midday: retreat to Soak City (included with admission) or grab Skyline Chili and LaRosa's Pizza, Cincinnati originals.
  5. The Beast at night is non-negotiable. Get in line 30 min before sunset. The 540° helix through the forest in darkness is unforgettable.

FAQ

What are the best days to visit Kings Island?

Tuesday and Wednesday are consistently the lightest crowd days. October Saturdays (Halloween Haunt) are the busiest days of the year. Late April and early May weekdays offer the shortest waits of the entire season.

Does Ride Ready use the park's official wait times?

Ride Ready tracks posted waits and trends so you can time rides better. If a park pauses updates or has downtime, your alerts may be delayed or temporarily less accurate.

What's the fastest way to get value on a one-day visit?

Pick 4-6 headliners, favorite them, and set a few "drop" alerts. When something dips, you pivot. That's where you claw back time without buying a line-skip add-on.
