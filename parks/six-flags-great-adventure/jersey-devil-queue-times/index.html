FAQ

Why is Jersey Devil's line so long?

Jersey Devil has one seat per row, which tanks throughput. Even with moderate demand, the line moves slowly because so few riders can board each cycle. That's why waits hit 35-45 minutes even on average days.

Is Jersey Devil scary?

Jersey Devil is intense but in a unique way. The single-seat, motorcycle-like exposed position makes you feel more vulnerable than a traditional coaster. Multiple inversions add to the thrill. It's not the tallest or fastest ride in the park, but the exposed feeling makes it feel more extreme than the stats suggest.

What's a single-rail coaster?

A single-rail coaster runs on a single rail instead of the traditional two-rail track. Jersey Devil is an RMC Raptor model. Riders sit one per row in a motorcycle-like position straddling the rail. It creates an incredibly exposed, open-air feeling with nothing beside you.
