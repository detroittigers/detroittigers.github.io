🗺️ Optimal Ride Order (Rope Drop Strategy)

Optimized to minimize total wait time across all headliners. The hourly data shows 10 AM is a universal sweet spot at Carowinds.

Fury 325 first. 16 min at open, doubles to 31 min by 3 PM. This is your biggest single time savings of the day.
10:30 AM
Copperhead Strike. 14 min at 10 AM, triples to 40 min by 4 PM. The most volatile ride in the park. Get it done early.
11:00 AM
Afterburn. Short walk from Copperhead Strike, only 14 min at 11 AM. Climbs to 26 min by 2 PM.
Midday
Thunder Striker, Hurler, and flats. Thunder Striker stays 20–23 min through midday, manageable. Hurler is similar. Work through the mid-tier rides.

5:00–7:00 PM · Sweet spot
Re-ride Fury 325 and Copperhead Strike. Both at 22–28 min in this window, before the closing rush sends them back up to 36–39 min at 9 PM.

🎢 Fury 325: Hour-by-Hour

The signature ride at Carowinds and the one most guests plan around. Here's what the data says about timing it right.

16 min at 10 AM
31 min peak at 3 PM
49 min 8 PM closing rush

Fury 325 has a unique pattern: it builds steadily through the afternoon, dips slightly at 5–6 PM (26–28 min), then spikes hard at 8 PM (49 min) before settling to 36 min at 9 PM. That 8 PM spike is the closing rush. Everyone wants "one last ride" on Fury.

The play: Ride Fury at 10 AM rope drop (16 min). If you want a second ride, hit it between 5–6 PM (26–28 min). Don't wait until 8 PM thinking the park will empty out. It's the worst hour of the day at 49 min.

🐍 Copperhead Strike: The Wild Card

Copperhead Strike is the most unpredictable ride at Carowinds. Its wait times swing wildly hour to hour, making it the ride that rewards planning the most.

14 min at 10 AM
40 min peak at 4 PM
39 min 9 PM closing rush

The swing from 14 min at 10 AM to 40 min at 4 PM is nearly triple. And unlike rides that ease up at close, Copperhead actually gets worse, jumping from 22 min at 5 PM to 39 min at 9 PM. The only reliable window is the first 90 minutes of the day or the brief 5 PM dip.

Don't save Copperhead for evening. It goes from 22 min at 5 PM to 39 min at 9 PM. That's a 77% increase. If you missed it at rope drop, your next best shot is the 5 PM window. After that, the closing rush takes over.
