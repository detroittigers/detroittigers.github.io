FAQ

What is Georgia Gold Rusher?

Georgia Gold Rusher is the world's first Intamin Ultra Surf coaster, a free-spinning gondola coaster that reaches 60 mph at 144 feet tall. It features gold rush theming and water features. There's no other ride like it on earth.

Is Georgia Gold Rusher a coaster?

Yes. Georgia Gold Rusher is classified as a coaster, specifically an Intamin Ultra Surf model. It uses free-spinning gondolas on a coaster track, creating a unique hybrid experience that combines coaster forces with unpredictable spinning.

When is the best time to ride Georgia Gold Rusher?

With only 65 historical data samples, we don't have enough for a reliable hourly breakdown. Based on general patterns, hit it early before novelty crowds build. As a world's-first ride, it attracts extra curiosity throughout the day.
