FAQ

When is Goliath shortest?

Goliath averages just 1 minute at 11 AM, essentially a walk-on. The line stays under 25 minutes through mid-afternoon but spikes dramatically in the evening, hitting 48 minutes at 9 PM.

Why is Goliath so long at closing?

Goliath has the most dramatic closing spike at Six Flags Over Georgia. The line jumps from 27 minutes at 6 PM to 48 minutes at 9 PM as guests rush for a last ride on the park's signature hyper coaster. Ride it early instead.

Is Goliath in the back of the park?

Yes. Goliath is in the back of Six Flags Over Georgia, which is why it's nearly empty at park open. Most guests haven't walked back yet. Head straight there at rope drop for a near walk-on.
