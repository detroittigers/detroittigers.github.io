Six Flags Over Georgia: Know When to Go

Track Six Flags Over Georgia wait times and set Ride Ready alerts for Goliath, Twisted Cyclone, Georgia Gold Rusher, Batman, Superman and more. Catch coaster windows without wasting your day in queues.

Quick park facts

LocationAustell, Georgia (west of Atlanta)
Best for12 coasters + included Hurricane Harbor water park + historic Monster Mansion dark ride
What to trackGoliath, Twisted Cyclone, Georgia Gold Rusher, Batman, Superman
The trickStart at the back of the park. The entrance funnels everyone forward, but the best rides (Goliath, Twisted Cyclone) are in the back.

Rides worth setting alerts for

Goliath

B&M Hyper coaster • pure airtime
200 ft70 mph6 camelback hills540° helix

This is the one people plan around. One of the best B&M hypers in the chain, and alerts help you ride it without sacrificing half the day to standby.

Fun fact: Repainted blue/gray for 2025, giving the ride a fresh look to match its legendary airtime.

Twisted Cyclone

RMC Hybrid • airtime machine
100 ft50 mph3 inversions10+ airtime moments

Compact but highly rated, and the enthusiast favorite. Moderate capacity means lines can build, so alerts are key.

Fun fact: Replaced Georgia Cyclone and packs more airtime per foot of track than most full-size coasters.

Georgia Gold Rusher

Intamin Ultra Surf • world's first
144 ft60 mphfree-spinning gondola

The world's first free-spinning gondola coaster (2025). Totally unique, available nowhere else on earth. High demand guaranteed.

Fun fact: A completely new ride type from Intamin. No other park has one yet.

Batman: The Ride

B&M Inverted • pure force
6 inversionsatmospheric effects

An unusually forceful installation of the classic B&M invert. A great backup when the headliners are peaking.

Fun fact: Recently got mist, lights, and atmospheric effects that make the queue and ride feel fresh.

Superman: Ultimate Flight

B&M Flying coaster • pretzel loop
pretzel loopsingle train ops

Single train operation means long waits are the norm. Alerts let you catch the dips instead of gambling on standby.

Fun fact: The second B&M flying coaster ever built, a pioneer of the ride type.

Tip: start conservative. Once you see how your day flows, tighten thresholds so you're only getting pings worth moving for.

If you only have one day

  1. Head straight to the back of the park at rope drop. Goliath and Twisted Cyclone first.
  2. Hit Dare Devil Dive early (lowest capacity in the park, lines stack fast).
  3. Georgia Gold Rusher mid-morning before novelty crowds peak.
  4. Use Monster Mansion and Justice League as air-conditioned afternoon breaks.
  5. Superman late afternoon when single-train waits ease up.

FAQ

Does Ride Ready use the park's official wait times?

Ride Ready tracks posted waits and trends so you can time rides better. If a park pauses updates or has downtime, your alerts may be delayed or temporarily less accurate.

What's the fastest way to get value on a one-day visit?

Pick 4–6 headliners, favorite them, and set a few "drop" alerts. When something dips, you pivot. That's where you claw back time without buying a line-skip add-on.
