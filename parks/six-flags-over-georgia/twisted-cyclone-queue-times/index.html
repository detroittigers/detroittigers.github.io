FAQ

Is Twisted Cyclone worth riding?

Yeah. Twisted Cyclone is an RMC hybrid coaster, compact but packed with 3 inversions and 10+ airtime moments. It's one of the highest-rated coasters at Six Flags Over Georgia despite its modest 100-foot height.

When is Twisted Cyclone shortest?

Twisted Cyclone averages just 2 minutes at 11 AM, essentially a walk-on. Unlike most SFOG rides, it actually drops back to 12 minutes at 9 PM, so closing time works here too.

What replaced Georgia Cyclone?

Twisted Cyclone replaced the original Georgia Cyclone in 2018. Rocky Mountain Construction (RMC) converted the wooden coaster into a steel-wood hybrid with inversions, airtime hills, and a dramatically smoother ride.
