Kings Dominion: Know When to Go

Track Kings Dominion wait times and set Ride Ready alerts for Pantherian, Rapterra, Twisted Timbers, Dominator, Flight of Fear and more. Catch coaster windows without wasting your day in queues.

Quick park facts

LocationDoswell, Virginia (between Richmond and D.C.)
Best forOne of the most intense giga coasters in North America + brand new world-record wing coaster
What to trackPantherian, Rapterra, Twisted Timbers, Dominator, Flight of Fear
The trickJuly weekdays are the park's best-kept secret. Shorter lines than spring break or Halloween Haunt.

Rides worth setting alerts for

Pantherian

Intamin Giga • pure intensity
305 ft90 mph85° drop

Honestly one of the most forceful giga coasters ever built. Alerts help you ride it without sacrificing half the day to standby.

Fun fact: Formerly Intimidator 305, renamed in 2025 with a jungle-cat theme for the new Jungle X-Pedition area.

Rapterra

B&M Launched Wing • world record
145 ft65 mph3,086 ft3 inversions

World's tallest and longest launched wing coaster. Still drawing crowds since its March 2025 opening, so alerts are essential.

Fun fact: 0–65 mph in 4 seconds. Opened as the world-record launched wing coaster.

Twisted Timbers

RMC Hybrid • barrel roll drop
111 ft54 mph

Enthusiast favorite tucked in the back of Jungle X-Pedition. Track it so you can catch it before the late-morning rush.

Fun fact: Built on the original Hurler structure. The barrel roll drop alone is worth the trip.

Dominator

B&M Floorless • 7 inversions
135 ft65 mph4,210 ft7 inversions

World's longest floorless coaster. High capacity means lines move fast, a great 'keep the day moving' ride.

Fun fact: At 4,210 ft, it's the longest floorless coaster on Earth.

Flight of Fear

Premier Rides indoor LIM launch • 4 inversions
54 mph0–54 in darkness4 inversions

Indoor launch + slow loading = brutal afternoon waits. Alerts help you catch those rare short windows.

Fun fact: Low capacity makes this one of the park's trickiest queues. Set alerts and be ready to move.

Tip: start conservative. Once you see how your day flows, tighten thresholds so you're only getting pings worth moving for.

If you only have one day

  1. Sprint to Rapterra at rope drop (newest, longest morning buzz).
  2. Pantherian immediately after. Get 1–2 rides before it hits 45+ min.
  3. Twisted Timbers next (back of Jungle X-Pedition, lines build by 10:30).
  4. Retreat to Soak City (included!) from noon–3pm on hot days.
  5. Flight of Fear and Dominator in the evening when lines drop.

FAQ

Does Ride Ready use the park's official wait times?

Ride Ready tracks posted waits and trends so you can time rides better. If a park pauses updates or has downtime, your alerts may be delayed or temporarily less accurate.

What's the fastest way to get value on a one-day visit?

Pick 4–6 headliners, favorite them, and set a few "drop" alerts. When something dips, you pivot. That's where you claw back time without buying a line-skip add-on.
