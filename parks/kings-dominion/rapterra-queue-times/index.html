FAQ

What records does Rapterra hold?

Rapterra holds the world records for tallest and longest launched wing coaster. At 145 feet tall and 3,086 feet long, it surpasses every other wing coaster with a launch system. It also launches from 0 to 65 mph in just 4 seconds.

When did Rapterra open?

Rapterra opened in March 2025 at Kings Dominion as part of the park's Jungle X-Pedition expansion.

Best seat on Rapterra?

The back left row is widely considered the best seat on Rapterra. Wing coasters give you an unobstructed view with nothing above or below you, and the back row amplifies the whip effect on every element. The left side gives the best view of the terrain.
