FAQ

What was Twisted Timbers before?

Twisted Timbers was built on the structure of the original Hurler wooden coaster. Rocky Mountain Construction (RMC) converted it into a steel-wood hybrid with a barrel roll drop, inversions, and a way smoother ride.

Is Twisted Timbers smooth?

Yes. Despite using the original Hurler wooden structure, the RMC conversion replaced the track with steel I-box rail. The result is a smooth, fast ride with none of the roughness of the original Hurler. RMC hybrids are known for being glass-smooth.

When should I ride Twisted Timbers?

Twisted Timbers averages around 24 minutes. You'll want to ride before 10:30 AM. The ride is in the back of the Jungle X-Pedition area, and lines tend to build by late morning as the crowd migrates deeper into the park.
