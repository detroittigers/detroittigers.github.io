  • Morning Advantage: The first 90 minutes offer the shortest waits of the day.

Key findings:

  • Mario Kart opens first every single day
  • Ministry typically opens 20-40 minutes after Mario Kart
  • Ministry isn't an official Early Entry ride, but it opens during EPA every day

The Strategy: Rope drop Mario Kart. By the time you're done (~85 min), Ministry just opened and you can head straight there.
