Early Entry Changed Feb 1

Universal switched early park admission at Epic Universe from individual rides to full land access.

BEFORE (JAN 9-31)
6 individual rides: Mario Kart, Yoshi, Stardust, Carousel, Monsters, Werewolf
NOW (FEB 1+)
Full land access: Ministry of Magic, Super Nintendo World, How to Train Your Dragon

This means Mine-Cart and Ministry are now available during early entry for the first time. Mario Kart has been posting 25-45 min at rope drop under the new system vs 35-60 min before.

Key Insights

We analyzed over 625,000 wait time samples to find the patterns that matter.

What the Data Shows (Updated February 4, 2026)

  • Sunday is the best day: 33 min average wait. Friday (41 min) and Thursday (42 min) are close behind. Avoid Tuesday (53 min).
  • Early entry changed Feb 1: Full land access replaced individual rides. Ministry, all of Super Nintendo World, and How to Train Your Dragon are now open during EPA.
  • Evening Window: Ministry drops from 150+ to ~45 min after 9 PM. Most rides drop 40-50% after 7 PM.
  • Morning Advantage: The first 90 minutes offer the shortest waits of the day (26 min average at 9 AM vs 73 min at noon).
  • Mine-Cart is the #2 hardest ride: 106 min average, nearly double Mario Kart (59 min). It posted a higher rope drop wait than Mario Kart every day this past week.

Which Ride to Rope Drop?

We've tracked rope drop waits every day since opening. Here's what the first few days under the new early entry look like (Feb 2-4):

Ride Mon 2/2 Tue 2/3 Wed 2/4
Ministry 210 240 90
Mine-Cart 160 150 30
Mario Kart 30 45 25
Stardust 60 90 15

Minutes. Feb 1 excluded (weather day). All times are first reported wait after ride opened.

What stands out:

  • Mario Kart during early entry is a bargain: 25-45 min at rope drop vs 100+ by midday. This is the clearest win from the new EPA.
  • Mine-Cart beats Mario Kart every day. It posted a higher rope drop wait than Mario Kart every single day this past week. It's the #2 hardest ride behind Ministry.
  • Ministry is still all over the place. 210 and 240 on Monday/Tuesday, then 90 on Wednesday. Too early to say if EPA is helping here.
  • Wednesday (2/4) was dramatically lighter across the board. Mine-Cart at 30, Mario Kart at 25. Could be a midweek effect.

The Strategy: During EPA, hit Mine-Cart first (highest all-day demand, 106 min avg), then Mario Kart right after (25-45 min at rope drop). Save Ministry for 7-9 PM when waits drop to ~45 min.

The 5 Golden Rules

1
Hit Mine-Cart first during EPA, then Mario Kart - Mine-Cart averages 106 min all day. Mario Kart is 25-45 min at rope drop during early entry. Do both before 10:30 AM.
2
Queue for Ministry by 7 PM - It averages 145-177 min from 10 AM-6 PM. By 9 PM it drops to 43 min. Don't wait too late though (queue may close early).
3
Sunday is the best day (33 min avg). Friday & Thursday are also great (41-42 min). Avoid Tuesday (53 min).
4
Monsters Unchained is a hidden gem - 16 min average, rarely over 45 min. One of the best dark rides in the park and almost always a walk-on.
5
Take time to explore the worlds - hidden details, unique food & photo ops in every land
