üö® The Reality Check

Here's something we didn't expect: Universal's own pricing signals suggest Presidents Day is NOT a calm weekend.

Universal is pricing Presidents Day at the same tier as peak Spring Break and Easter.

Their dynamic pricing puts this weekend in their highest crowd-expectation bracket. The week AFTER Presidents Day (Feb 22-27) drops significantly.

Why this matters: Universal uses historical data and forward bookings to set these tiers. When they price a weekend at peak levels, they're expecting peak crowds. Valentine's Day + Presidents Day + limited off-peak options = recipe for busy.

The silver lining: MLK data showed Monday can still be 28-31% lighter than Saturday. Even in a busy weekend, there's a better day. Read on.

ü§î What's Still True About February

Not everything is doom and gloom. Some factors still work in your favor:

  • Park Maturity: Epic Universe has been open for 7+ months. Operations are smoother than opening year jitters.
  • Weather: Expect 70s and low humidity, perfect for all-day touring.
  • No Major School Breaks: Most districts are in session (Presidents Day is just Monday off).

But "better than Christmas" doesn't mean "calm." Set expectations accordingly.

üìä What MLK Weekend Showed Us

We just tracked 52,693 wait time samples across MLK weekend (Jan 17-19). Here's what we learned that applies to Presidents Day:

The big finding: Holiday Monday was the best day.

Not a wildcard. Not "about the same." The actual federal holiday had the lowest waits across all three parks. Epic dropped 28% from Saturday to Monday. IOA dropped 31%.

‚öîÔ∏è The Strategy

This is NOT a "relaxed touring" weekend. Universal's pricing signals say bring your A-game. Here's how to work it:

Best play: Monday only, rope drop. MLK data showed 28-31% lighter than Saturday. Get there before park open, hit headliners first two hours, then you've got flexibility.
If you're doing multiple days: Flip the script - do lower-tier rides Saturday evening (when couples are doing dinner), then hit Epic Universe hard Monday morning for the headliners.
Alternative: Skip this weekend entirely. Universal's pricing shows Feb 22-27 (the week after) drops significantly. If your schedule allows, that week is actually the "calm February" window we thought this weekend was.

  • Weather: One upside - 70s and low humidity means outdoor queue tolerance is higher.
