Epic Universe
Spring Break Survival Guide

Spring break is the busiest stretch of the year at Epic Universe. Here's the data-backed strategy to beat the crowds — and the app that runs it in real time.

1Rope drop Mine-Cart Madness first

Mine-Cart Madness consistently hits 90–140 min waits by 10 AM on spring break days. It's on the Early Park Admission list — arrive by 8 AM for sub-30-min waits. This is the single biggest time save of the day.

2Hit Ministry of Laughter after 7 PM

Ministry waits regularly drop from 100–150 min down to 30–45 min in the final two hours of operation. Spring break crowds pile in at open and thin out by evening. Save Harry Potter for last.

3Use a drop alert instead of refreshing wait times

Set a wait-time alert for your target ride in Ride Ready and put your phone away. We'll ping you the moment it crosses your threshold — no obsessive refreshing required.

4Plan your day the night before

Spring break crowds are predictable. Use Ride Ready's AI day planner the night before to build a touring order based on your group's height restrictions and ride preferences. Walk in with a plan.
