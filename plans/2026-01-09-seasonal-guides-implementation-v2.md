### Universal Strategies (Every Week)

1. **Early Entry (EPA)** - 1 hour before general public, mandatory during peak
2. **Evening Window (7 PM - Close)** - Waits drop 40-60%
3. **Midday Escape** - Leave 11 AM - 4 PM, return refreshed
4. **Single Rider Lines** - Where available, use them
5. **Virtual Queues** - Book immediately when available

### Sample Itineraries

1. **1-Day Sprint (Peak Week)** - Aggressive, EPA required, midday break, evening push
2. **2-Day Balanced (Any Week)** - Day 1 headliners, Day 2 relaxed exploration
3. **3-Day Cruise (Lighter Weeks)** - One land per day, evening headliners

## Brand Voice Reminders

Every guide must sound like:
- A helpful friend who knows the parks
- Someone who's been there, not read about it
- Data-informed but not robotic
- Honest about limitations ("we might be wrong")

Avoid:
- Corporate speak ("We're excited to announce...")
- AI tells (em dashes, "Great question!", excessive exclamation marks)
- Hedging everything ("might possibly perhaps")
- Overselling the app (1-2 mentions max)

**Read aloud test:** If it sounds like a press release, rewrite it.
