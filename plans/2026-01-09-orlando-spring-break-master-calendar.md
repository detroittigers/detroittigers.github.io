# Orlando Spring Break 2026: Master Regional Calendar

**Created:** January 9, 2026
**Purpose:** Comprehensive spring break calendar for all Orlando parks
**Usable for:** Epic Universe, Disney World, Universal Studios, SeaWorld, etc.

---

## Executive Summary

Spring break at Orlando parks runs roughly **March 9 - April 24, 2026** (7 weeks).

**The worst week: March 16-20, 2026 (Week 2)**
- Florida locals + Ontario Canada + Dallas/Fort Worth + Indianapolis = COLLISION

**The best weeks:**
- Week 1 (March 9-13): Only Houston + early starters
- Week 7 (April 20-24): Only Boston stragglers

## Strategic Recommendations by Market

### For Florida Residents
```
Your break (March 16-20) is THE WORST WEEK.

If you have annual passes: Skip your break week. Visit March 14-15
(before) or March 21-22 (after) instead.

If you must go during break: Tuesday/Wednesday slightly lighter
than weekend.
```

### For Michigan Families
```
Your break (late March/early April) misses the worst crowds.

You arrive as Northeast is finishing and Florida is long gone.
Week 5-6 crowds are manageable.

Sunday still wins. Standard strategy works.
```

### For Texas Families
```
Houston (Week 1): You lucked out. Lightest week of spring break.

Dallas/Fort Worth (Week 2): You're in the collision. Read the
survival guide. Consider Express Pass.
```

### For Northeast Families
```
You hit Week 4-5, after Florida locals leave.

Crowds are elevated but not insane. Pack patience.
NYC gets a long break (April 2-10) - go early in your window
when crowds are slightly lighter.
```

### For Chicago Families
```
You hit Week 3, the "second wave" after Florida clears.

Miami-Dade families are your main competition. Cincinnati too.
Better than Week 2, but still aggressive strategy needed.
```

### For Atlanta/Charlotte Families
```
Week 5 is your friend. Post-Easter, most spring breakers gone.

You're sharing with Columbus and tail-end Michigan.
Standard strategy works. Sleep in. Enjoy.
```

### For Boston Families
```
Week 7 = BEST TIMING.

You're alone. Everyone else went home weeks ago.
Parks are nearly normal. All days are good days.
Congratulations on your school calendar.
```

### For Canadian Families
```
Ontario March Break (Week 2) is the collision.

You share the week with Florida locals, Dallas, and Indy.
Expect peak crowds. Plan aggressively.

Consider: Fly down Week 1 or Week 3 instead of your actual
break if you have flexibility (use vacation days around it).
```

## University/College Spring Breaks

College students travel differently than K-12 families:
- More flexible with timing (skip classes before/after)
- Travel in groups, hit parks later in the day
- More likely to buy Express Pass (splitting costs)
- Stay later at night

### Major University Calendar

| University | Spring Break 2026 | Week # | Notes |
|------------|-------------------|--------|-------|
| **University of Michigan** | Feb 28 - Mar 8 | Pre-Week 1 | EARLIEST - before spring break season |
| Penn State | Mar 9-13 | Week 1 | Starts the wave |
| University of Florida | Mar 16-20 | Week 2 | Home state collision |
| Florida State | Mar 16-20 | Week 2 | Home state collision |
| UCF | Mar 16-21 | Week 2 | Home state collision |
| Ohio State | Mar 16-20 | Week 2 | Major Midwest school |
| UT Austin | Mar 16-21 | Week 2 | Texas adds to Week 2 |
| NYU | Mar 16-20 | Week 2 | Northeast elite |
| USC | Mar 15-22 | Week 2 | California travelers |
| Georgia Tech | Mar 23-27 | Week 3 | Atlanta area |
| UCLA/UC schools | Mar 21-Apr 4 | Weeks 3-4 | Quarter system = later |

### Key Insights

**Week 2 is even worse than K-12 suggests:**
Most major universities (UF, FSU, UCF, OSU, UT Austin, NYU, USC) all hit March 16-20. This compounds the K-12 collision with Florida + Dallas + Ontario + Indy.

**University of Michigan is the outlier:**
Their Feb 28 - Mar 8 break is BEFORE spring break season even starts. Michigan students get the park nearly to themselves.

**Quarter system schools (UCLA, UC Berkeley, etc.) are later:**
Their breaks fall in late March/early April (Weeks 3-4), spreading out California visitors.
