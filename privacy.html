Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Ride Ready ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App") and related services ("Service").

By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information:

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Username and profile preferences
  • Age range and accessibility needs
  • Ride preferences and thrill level settings

User-Generated Content:

  • Reviews and ratings you submit
  • Photos you choose to share
  • Comments and feedback
  • Trip plans and saved favorites

Communication:

  • Messages you send to customer support
  • Survey responses and feedback
  • Beta testing feedback

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Location Information:

  • Precise location data (when location services are enabled)
  • Park visit detection and queue location tracking
  • Movement patterns within theme parks
  • Time spent at different locations

Usage Data:

  • App features you use and frequency of use
  • Time spent in the App and session duration
  • Screens viewed and user interactions
  • Search queries and preferences
  • Crash reports and performance data

Device Information:

  • Device type, model, and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address and mobile network information
  • Language and timezone settings
  • Push notification tokens

2.3 Information from Third Parties

Theme Park Data:

  • Publicly available wait times and ride status
  • Park hours and special events
  • Ride closures and maintenance schedules

Analytics Services:

  • App usage analytics and user behavior insights
  • Crash reporting and performance monitoring
  • A/B testing and feature usage data

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core Service Features

Personalized Recommendations:

  • Analyze your ride preferences and visit history
  • Suggest optimal park touring plans
  • Provide personalized wait time alerts
  • Customize content based on your interests

Location-Based Services:

  • Detect when you're in queue for rides
  • Provide location-relevant notifications
  • Track park visits for analytics
  • Enable automatic check-ins and queue timing

Queue Management:

  • Send push notifications for wait time changes
  • Provide smart queue alerts based on your preferences
  • Track queue times for popular rides
  • Suggest optimal ride timing

3.2 Service Improvement

App Enhancement:

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Identify and fix bugs and performance issues
  • Test new features with user segments
  • Optimize app performance and user experience

Data Analytics:

  • Generate aggregated usage statistics
  • Understand popular rides and parks
  • Improve recommendation algorithms
  • Research user behavior trends

3.3 Communication

Service Communications:

  • Send push notifications for queue alerts
  • Provide app updates and new feature announcements
  • Deliver customer support responses
  • Send important service notifications

Marketing (Optional):

  • Send promotional content about new features
  • Share relevant theme park information
  • Provide seasonal recommendations and tips
  • Offer premium subscription information

5.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication and access controls

  • Employee training on data protection

5.2 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Account data is retained until you delete your account, after which it is deleted within 30 days.

6.1 Access and Control

  • Manage notification settings
  • Control location sharing preferences
  • Delete user-generated content

Data Access:

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • View your activity history and preferences
  • Access data we've collected about your app usage

6.2 Data Portability

You can request a machine-readable copy of your data to transfer to another service.

You can delete your account at any time through the App settings. This will permanently remove your personal information from our systems within 30 days.

  • Contacting customer support

6.5 Location Data Control

Disable Location Services:

  • Turn off location access through device settings
  • Use the App without location-based features
  • Delete location history through App settings

7.1 Age Requirements

Ride Ready is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. We use age verification during account creation to help enforce this policy. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

7.2 Parental Control

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately so we can delete such information.

7.3 Teen Users (13-17)

For users aged 13-17, we recommend parental guidance and supervision when using location-based features.
