Best Time to Visit Each Park

Epic Universe Crowd Calendar

Opened May 2025 and already drawing massive crowds. Weekdays run 30-40% lighter than weekends, and surprisingly Sunday is actually the best weekend day to go. Skip summer and spring break (expect 8-10 scores). Best months: September and January.

Universal Studios Florida Crowd Calendar

Wednesday and Thursday are consistently the lightest days of the week here. Avoid holiday weekends at all costs. Best months to visit: September, early October, and late January.

Islands of Adventure Crowd Calendar

Wed and Thu are your best bets, same pattern as Universal Studios Florida. Epic Universe opening has pushed some spillover crowds here too, so plan accordingly. Best months: September and late January.

SeaWorld Orlando Crowd Calendar

Generally runs lighter than Universal, making it a smart alternative on busy Orlando weeks. Wed and Thu are still the lightest days, though summer camps push weekday crowds up in June and July. Best months: September and February.

Busch Gardens Tampa Crowd Calendar

Weekdays are dramatically lighter than weekends here. HowlOScream drives big fall crowds on weekends, so stick to weekdays if you're visiting in October. Best months: September weekdays and January.

Busch Gardens Williamsburg Crowd Calendar

Open March through December, and weekdays here are basically walk-on territory. HowlOScream in the fall brings heavy weekend crowds, but early spring is genuinely quiet. Best months: early spring and September weekdays.

Cedar Point Crowd Calendar

The gap between weekdays and weekends here is dramatic. HalloWeekends in the fall packs Saturdays, so avoid those if you can. Best months: May weekdays and September Tuesdays, which are the quietest days of the whole season.

Kings Island Crowd Calendar

Near Cincinnati and open April through October. Weekdays are much lighter than weekends, and Haunt drives big fall weekend crowds. Best months to visit: April weekdays and September.

Kings Dominion Crowd Calendar

Open March through November near Richmond, VA. Follows a very similar pattern to Kings Island -- weekdays are consistently lighter. Best months: early spring weekdays and September.

Carowinds Crowd Calendar

Straddles the NC/SC border just south of Charlotte and runs March through December. Weekdays are your lightest option, and SCarowinds brings heavy fall weekend traffic. Best months: April weekdays and September.

Canada's Wonderland Crowd Calendar

Just north of Toronto and open May through October. Weekdays are dramatically quieter than weekends throughout the season. Halloween Haunt fills fall weekends, so plan around it. Best months: May weekdays and September.

Six Flags Over Georgia Crowd Calendar

Near Atlanta and open March through November. Weekdays are the lightest option all season, with Fright Fest pushing fall weekends to some of the busiest days of the year. Best months: early spring and September.

Six Flags Great Adventure Crowd Calendar

In Jackson, NJ and open March through December. Fright Fest here is genuinely massive and makes October weekends some of the most packed in the region. Best months: April weekdays and September.

Six Flags Great America Crowd Calendar

Just north of Chicago near Gurnee, IL, open April through October. Fright Fest drives heavy fall weekend crowds. Weekdays are the move all season long. Best months: May weekdays and September.
